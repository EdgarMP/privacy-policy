Apptegy Inc. will never ask Users of the Services to provide personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address and phone number (“Personal Information”).

The Services are not intended for use by children under 13 without parental supervision. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Information from children under 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.
If you suspect that information from a child has been inappropriately collected or handled through the Services, please contact our data protection officer at josiah@apptegy.com. Children under 18 may request removal of content or Personal Information that has been posted on the Services.
